Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

Northwind Financials Inc. reported total revenue of $42.3 billion for the fiscal year ended
December 31, 2024, representing a 12% increase compared to $37.8 billion in fiscal year 2023.

Net income for fiscal year 2024 was $6.1 billion, or $4.87 per diluted share, compared to
$5.4 billion, or $4.32 per diluted share, in fiscal year 2023. The increase reflects strong
performance across all business segments and disciplined cost management.

Operating income increased to $8.2 billion in fiscal year 2024, up from $7.1 billion in the
prior year, resulting in an operating margin of 19.4%.

Item 1A. Risk Factors

The company faces significant competition in all markets in which it operates. Competitive
pressures may adversely affect pricing, market share, and results of operations.

Changes in interest rates, foreign currency exchange rates, and commodity prices could
materially affect the company's financial condition and results of operations.
